Website UMKM : Warung Sembako Bu Siti

This project focused on creating a user-friendly website for Warung Sembako Bu Siti, a small business offering grocery and essential products. The aim was to establish an online presence for the warung, allowing customers to browse products, check prices, and make orders easily. The website features a simple and clean design, highlighting key products, contact information, and location. It was optimized for mobile devices to ensure accessibility for local customers, helping the business expand its reach.
